Welcome to panda ʕ•ᴥ•ʔ

A new, basic, lazy as a panda, easy-to-use css library.

  1. Import from: https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/dodgerbluelab/panda@latest/css/styles.css
  2. Simply overwrite the --panda-clr-pri and --panda-clr-sec in :root {} of your own css style sheet to set your primary and secondary UI color.
  3. If you're unhappy with the sizing of things, just overwrite --panda-scale.

Now the entire scaling and spacings system will look awesome as its based of the Fibonnaci sequenze.

To style buttons and inputs, panda uses data-panda to configure different styles of the markup. Have a look at the buttons section.
